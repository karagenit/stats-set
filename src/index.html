Stats Set

In this game, you are trying to match together "sets" of three cards. Each card has three attributes: shape (histogram, dot plot, box plot), color (orange, blue, purple), and number (one, two, or three). For three cards to constitute a "set", the cards must have each of their attributes be the same or be different.
